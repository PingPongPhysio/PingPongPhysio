My Mission

To help individuals stay active and injury-free by combining my physiotherapy knowledge with my professional experience in table tennis. I achieve this through:

• Injury prevention: Enhancing a strong technique foundation using the fastest joints and timing to create efficient shots.

• Simple Coaching: Breaking down complex biomechanics into easy-to-follow steps.

• Long-Term Health: My goal isn’t just to teach you a stroke, it is to help you build a lifelong, active habit that keeps your body and mind sharp.

Why Work With Me?

My coaching plans is built on three core pillars:

• Clinical Expertise: With a BSc in Sports Rehabilitation and an MSc in Physiotherapy, I have a deep understanding of human anatomy and injury prevention.

• High-Level Experience: Having reached a high level in table tennis myself, I understand the technical demands, footwork patterns, and mental stamina required to succeed.

• Biomechanic Analysis: I don't just teach "the stroke”, I analyse your movement. Using biomechanical principles, I develop strategies to make the sport easier to understand, more efficient, and physically sustainable.
