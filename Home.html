Sports Massage

Home visits only

Recovery Sports Massage is a specialised form of massage therapy designed to improve performance, reduce risk of injury and expedite recovery. Whether you are a professional athlete, a fitness enthusiast, or simply enjoy an active lifestyle, sports massage can offer multiple benefits tailored to your specific needs.

Physiotherapy

Home visits only

Personalised care with a thorough assessment and diagnosis, objectively measuring your balance, strength and movement patterns. This allows for specific tailoring of hands-on treatment (such as massage and mobilisations) and a rehabilitation plan. You will receive education on the underlying causes of your issues and be guided with high-level support to prevent future problems.
